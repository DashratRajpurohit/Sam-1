Delhi



Delhi is the capital of India and a city full of history and culture. It is known for its monuments, markets, and diversity.


Famous places include the Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Lotus Temple. Old Delhi is popular for its food and bazaars.


Today, Delhi is also a modern city with metros, malls, and opportunities, making it a lively mix of old and new.
