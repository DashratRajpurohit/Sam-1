Jaipur[The Pink City]



Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is also known as the "Pink City" for its unique pink-colored buildings. It is famous for its royal heritage and culture.


The city has stunning landmarks like Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace. Its bazaars are filled with handicrafts, jewelry, and traditional clothes.


Jaipur is part of India's Golden Triangle and blends history with modern development, making it a popular tourist destination.
