Mumbai



Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a bustling city known as the "City of Dreams." It is famous for its energy, diversity, and fast-paced life.


Landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Elephanta Caves attract visitors from around the world. Bollywood, India's film industry, also calls Mumbai its home.


Along with skyscrapers and luxury, Mumbai has vibrant street food, local trains, and beaches, making it a city that never sleeps.
